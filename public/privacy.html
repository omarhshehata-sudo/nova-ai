Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

We believe in transparency. This policy explains how Nova AI collects, uses, and protects your data.

Effective date: April 9, 2026 · Last updated: April 9, 2026

👋 1. Introduction

Welcome to Nova AI. Nova AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at nova-ai-blue.vercel.app and provides AI-powered chat services (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform or use our Service.

By accessing or using Nova AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not access the Service.

📦 2. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways depending on how you interact with our Service:

Account Information

  • Name and display name you provide during account creation
  • Email address used for authentication
  • Profile picture you upload or import from a third-party provider
  • Authentication credentials managed through our identity provider (Supabase) or third-party OAuth services (GitHub, Google)

User-Generated Content

  • Messages and prompts you submit to the AI assistant
  • Conversation history stored locally in your browser
  • Profile customizations and preferences you configure

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information — browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, timestamps, and interaction patterns
  • IP address — collected by our hosting provider for security and analytics purposes
  • Referral data — how you arrived at our platform

⚙️ 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service — including authenticating your identity, personalizing your experience, and enabling core features
  • To process AI interactions — your messages are processed to generate relevant AI-powered responses
  • To improve the Service — understanding usage patterns helps us enhance features, fix issues, and develop new capabilities
  • To communicate with you — sending service-related notices, security alerts, and support messages
  • To ensure security — detecting, preventing, and addressing fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations — fulfilling requirements under applicable laws and regulations

🤖 4. AI Data Processing

Important: When you use Nova AI, the messages and prompts you submit are processed by AI models to generate responses. Please be mindful of the information you share in your conversations.
  • Your inputs (messages, prompts, and queries) are sent to AI language models to produce responses in real time
  • Conversation history is stored locally in your browser's storage — we do not retain your chat history on our servers
  • We do not use your personal conversations to train or improve AI models
  • AI-generated responses are provided for informational and assistive purposes only and may not always be accurate, complete, or current
  • We recommend that you do not share sensitive personal information (such as passwords, financial details, or health records) in your conversations with the AI

🍪 5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Nova AI uses minimal tracking technologies to provide and improve the Service:

  • Local Storage — we store your authentication state, user profile, and conversation history in your browser's localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers
  • Session Cookies — our authentication provider (Supabase) may use cookies to manage your login session securely
  • Hosting Analytics — our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect standard web analytics data including page views, request logs, and performance metrics

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party behavioral tracking. You can clear your local data at any time through your browser settings.

🔗 6. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to operate our platform. Each has its own privacy policy governing data they process:

  • Supabase — authentication, user management, and identity services (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — web hosting, deployment, and edge network infrastructure (Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub — OAuth authentication for sign-in (Privacy Statement)
  • Google — OAuth authentication for sign-in (Privacy Policy)

We carefully select providers that maintain high standards for data protection and security. We only share the minimum information necessary for each service to function.

🛡️ 7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We will never sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — with trusted third-party services that help us operate the platform (as listed above), bound by contractual obligations to protect your data
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, subpoena, court order, or government request
  • Safety and security — to protect the rights, safety, or property of Nova AI, our users, or the public
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case your data would remain subject to this Privacy Policy

🗄️ 8. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

  • Account data — retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service
  • Conversation history — stored locally in your browser and fully under your control. You can delete it at any time by clearing your browser storage
  • Usage and analytics data — retained in aggregated, anonymized form for service improvement
  • Authentication logs — retained by our identity provider (Supabase) according to their retention policies

When you delete your account or request data deletion, we will remove your personal information from our active systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

🔒 9. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL encryption
  • Authentication is handled through industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocols and secure session management
  • We use Supabase's enterprise-grade infrastructure for identity management, which includes encryption at rest and in transit
  • Our application is hosted on Vercel's secure edge network with built-in DDoS protection and security headers
  • Sensitive conversation data is stored locally on your device, reducing attack surface and server-side risk

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously monitor and improve our security practices.

⚖️ 10. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Data portability — request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time
  • Revoke OAuth access — you can disconnect GitHub or Google access directly from your account settings on those platforms

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

👶 11. Children's Privacy

Nova AI is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our systems promptly.
