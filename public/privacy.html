Privacy Policy

Last updated — April 9, 2026

📋 Overview

Nova AI ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service at nova-ai-blue.vercel.app.

📦 Information We Collect

  • Account information — email address, display name, and profile picture when you sign up
  • Authentication data — OAuth tokens from GitHub or Google used solely to verify your identity
  • Chat data — conversations are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as page views may be collected by our hosting provider

🔧 How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your account
  • To personalize your experience (display name, profile picture)
  • To persist your chat history across sessions (stored locally)
  • To improve and maintain the service

🔒 Data Storage & Security

Your chat conversations are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage — we do not store them on any server. Authentication is handled securely through Supabase and third-party OAuth providers (GitHub, Google). We use industry-standard practices to protect your data.

🤝 Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and user management
  • GitHub — OAuth sign-in
  • Google — OAuth sign-in
  • Vercel — hosting and deployment

Each provider has its own privacy policy governing the data they process.

🗑️ Your Rights

  • You can delete your local data at any time by clearing your browser storage
  • You can revoke OAuth access through your GitHub or Google account settings
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us

✉️ Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, reach us through our GitHub repository at github.com/omarhshehata-sudo/nova-ai.
